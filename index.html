Frequently Asked Questions

Any questions? We've answered the most common ones below!

What is the Trading Bonus Credit promotion?

The Trading Bonus Credit promotion rebates your trading commissions on eligible Forex and Gold lots in the form of trading bonus credit. For every 1 standard lot traded, you receive $7 USD in bonus credit, capped at 100 lots ($700 USD total). The rebate is calculated and credited to your account after the campaign ends.

Is the promotion available for all trading products?

No. This promotion applies to Forex and Gold products only.

Do I need to deposit beforehand? Can I withdraw during the promotion?

There is no minimum deposit requirement to participate. However, you will need a funded trading account to trade. You may withdraw funds during the promotion period, as eligibility is based solely on your eligible trading volume. The maximum rebate is capped at 100 lots ($700 USD).

When will I receive my trading bonus credit?

Your trading bonus credit will be calculated and reviewed after the campaign ends (30 April 2026). Once approved, the bonus will typically be credited to your trading account within a few business days.

Which countries are eligible for this promotion?

This promotion is open to TMGM retail clients registered in: Germany, Netherlands, Italy, Belgium, Poland, Croatia, Czech Republic, Turkey, Greece, Nigeria, Kenya, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Jordan, Bahrain, Kuwait, and Oman.

Can I cash out the bonus credit?

No, the trading bonus credit cannot be cashed out. However, it can be used as margin for your trades, and your trading volume will be accumulated as points for TMGM Rewards.
